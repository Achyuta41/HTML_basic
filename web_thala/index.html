Padma bhusan Ajith kumar

Actor | Racer | Philanthropist

Ajith Kumar is a renowned Indian actor primarily working in Tamil cinema. Known for his versatility and charismatic screen presence, he has acted in numerous successful films and has a massive fan following. Apart from acting, Ajith is also a professional car racer and a passionate philanthropist, actively supporting various charitable causes. His dedication, humility, and achievements have made him one of the most respected personalities in the South Indian film industry.
